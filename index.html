About Elle

Empowering Voice, Illuminating Presence.

Elle Logan is a multi-disciplinary artist and voice educator specializing in embodied vocal technique and authentic self-expression. As founder of the Embodied Voice Institute, Elle cultivates transformational experiences that blend classical technique and therapeutic embodiment work. As co-director of the Lift The Spirit Choir, she weaves community through the power of song.

Classically trained with a Master of Music in Operatic Performance from the University of North Texas, Elle has performed extensively at home and abroad. During her time as an Educational Artist with The Dallas Opera, Elle was a part of the team that brings music education to over 25,000 children per year. Her work now focuses on guiding singers toward vocal freedom through embodied awareness, technical excellence, and somatic expression.

As a photographer, Elle has evolved from creating brand and portrait photography for artists and arts organizations to exploring the slower, more contemplative practice of film photography. This shift reflects a deepening commitment to presence, intentionality, and the beauty found in slowing down—values that now deeply shape her life.

What we create together is rooted in deep listening—to your voice, your body, your truth. Whether holding space through voice work or through the lens, it's all about the same thing: helping you come home to yourself.
